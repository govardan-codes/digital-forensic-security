About the Project

The system enforces secure onboarding and role-based access for Admin, Investigator, and Verifier. Each piece of evidence is signed, hashed, and time-stamped during upload, then anchored to an immutable ledger for non-repudiation. Our Optimised Encryption balances speed with security, ensuring large files remain responsive to handle while maintaining confidentiality.
