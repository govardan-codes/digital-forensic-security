🧩 Verify Digital Evidence

Upload a digital evidence file to validate its authenticity through blockchain-based integrity verification.

📁
Click to choose a file or drag & drop it here
Supported: images, PDFs, text, and archives

⚙️ How It Works

  1. Upload the evidence file (any supported format).
  2. The system computes a SHA-256 hash of your uploaded file.
  3. It retrieves the original hash from the blockchain ledger.
  4. If both hashes match → ✅ Authentic.
  5. If they differ → ⚠️ Tampered.
  6. The result is permanently recorded on the blockchain.

Every verification is logged with your identity, timestamp, and result for full transparency.
